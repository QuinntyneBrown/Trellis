The gallery documents the Diagram Wizard rather than the whole app; see README.md for the mock-to-component mapping and fidelity notes. Diagram Wizard

These nine pages mock the shipped Diagram Wizard: a side panel that builds C4 or sequence diagrams one guided step at a time, writing PlantUML into the editor and re-rendering the preview after every action (one-way: wizard → diagram; the editor stays hand-editable throughout). They are the gallery's only pages — the older light-theme mocks documented a shell the app has retired and were deleted. They mirror the app's current shell and are therefore dark, styled by assets/mock-dark.css, hand-compiled from today's component SCSS and the --tds-* tokens in frontend/src/styles/tokens.scss. They carry the hamburger rail, Explain This toggle and title-bar copy button, plus the wizard rail icon. (The rendered diagram stays on a white plate by design, even on the dark pane — that is what the app itself does, so exports keep their background.) Element ids and participant aliases in the generated code are auto-derived from the names, so the ids shown here are illustrative.
