Indicators

Everything that reports state without asking for input: the connection dot, spinners at three sizes, the error banner and its toast placement, and the kind badge.

Kind badge

Derived from: the documents tree's kind badge / templates-panel__kind-badge — .tds-badge.

PUML MD

0.6rem bold uppercase chip on the badge surface. It states a document kind — it is informational, never interactive, and never a status color.

Rules

Do Show backend error text verbatim in banners (pre-wrap preserves its formatting) — the message is the diagnostic.
Do Keep the sr-only status text in sync with the dot; the dot alone is invisible to assistive tech.
Don't Auto-dismiss error toasts on a timer — save failures stay until acknowledged or resolved.
Don't Introduce success toasts; success is shown by the result itself (the rendered diagram, the updated tree).
