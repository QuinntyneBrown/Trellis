Panels

The docked side panel is one component worn four ways — Documents (virtual-folder tree), Explorer (disk tree), Templates (flat catalog), and Explain This (prompt wizard). Same chrome surface, same uppercase header, same empty-state voice; only the content differs.

Resize divider

Derived from: resize-divider / pixel-resize-divider — .tds-divider.

Panes are separated by a 1px seam with an invisible 9px grab area centered on it. The seam takes the accent color on hover and during a drag, and the document body gets cursor: col-resize with text selection suppressed while dragging. ARIA: role="separator", aria-orientation="vertical", with aria-valuenow/min/max reflecting the pane size.

Left seam resting; right seam in its hover/drag accent state.

Rules

Do Keep both side panels mounted and stacked; visibility toggles display, never re-creates state.
Do Put panel-scoped actions in the header as tree-action buttons — not as text buttons, not floating in content.
Don't Let a panel exceed its 170–500px slot or gain internal horizontal scrolling; names truncate instead.
Don't Invent a fourth panel chrome — new tools wear the same header, surface, and empty-state voice.
