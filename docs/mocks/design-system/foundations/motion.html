Motion

Motion in Trellis is scarce and purely informative: a tooltip fading in, a twisty rotating, a spinner while work is in flight. Hover fills, pane resizing, and dialogs are instant — the workbench never animates layout.

Tokens

Derived from: rail-button (tooltip 0.12s ease), _tree-row.scss (chevron 0.1s ease), loading-spinner / file-tree-node (0.7s linear infinite).

TokenValueUse
--tds-motion-tooltip0.12s easeRail tooltip opacity/visibility
--tds-motion-twisty0.1s easeTree chevron rotation (0° → 90°)
--tds-motion-spin0.7s linear, infiniteAll spinners (page, overlay, in-row)

Specimens

hover: tooltip fades in over 0.12s
twisty: collapsed / expanded (0.1s)
Rendering diagram… spinner: 0.7s/rev, linear

Progress

In-flight work always shows the spinner, sized to context: the shared .tds-spinner with a label in panels, the small overlay spinner pinned to the preview's top-right corner during re-renders (.tds-preview__spinner-overlay), and the 0.75rem in-row spinner while a tree directory loads. Spinners appear immediately — there is no artificial delay — and are removed the moment the result (or its error) lands.

Reduced motion

The only continuous animation is the spinner; tooltip and twisty transitions are ≤ 120ms. Implementations should disable the twisty rotation and tooltip fade under prefers-reduced-motion: reduce; spinners may keep rotating — they carry state, and their removal would lose meaning.

Do Use the three motion tokens as-is; new transitions require a new token and a documented reason.
Don't Animate layout (widths, heights, positions) anywhere in the workbench.
