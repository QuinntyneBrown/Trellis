Elevation

On a dark theme, height reads primarily through surface lightening and hairline borders; shadows are kept for the two genuinely floating layers (pop-outs and modals). The z-index contract is small and closed.

Layers

Derived from: the light theme's shadow pair (0 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,.12) pop-outs; 0 8px 24px rgba(0,0,0,.2) dialogs), re-weighted for dark.

Hairlines

Structure is drawn with 1px hairlines, never with gaps or heavy strokes: --tds-border (#2b2b2b) separates chrome regions (panel seams, headers, the title bar's bottom edge); --tds-border-strong (#3c3c3c) outlines interactive controls (inputs, buttons, pills) and floating layers.
