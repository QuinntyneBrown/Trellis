# Code of Conduct

Trellis is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free project environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and professional language.
- Assume good intent, but be precise when discussing technical concerns.
- Give and accept constructive feedback.
- Respect maintainers' time by keeping discussions focused and actionable.
- Credit the work and ideas of others where appropriate.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Personal attacks or comments that are unrelated to the work.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make participation unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately when appropriate, and focused on protecting the project community.

If you need to report a conduct concern, contact a maintainer privately when possible. If a private channel is not available, open a minimal public issue that asks for a maintainer contact without including sensitive details.
